# Instagram Visual Templates for The Stoic Seller

## Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Blue (#1e3a8a) - Trust, wisdom, stability
- **Secondary**: Gold (#d4af37) - Premium, value, ancient wisdom
- **Accent**: Light Gray (#f8fafc) - Clean, professional
- **Text**: Dark Gray (#1f2937) - Readable, professional

### Typography
- **Headers**: Bold, sans-serif fonts (Helvetica, Arial)
- **Body**: Clean, readable fonts
- **Quotes**: Serif fonts for classical feel (Times, Georgia)

### Logo Elements
- Stoic column icon
- "S" monogram in circle
- Classical Greek/Roman aesthetic

## Post Templates

### Template 1: Quote Posts
```
Background: Deep blue gradient
Layout: 
- Stoic quote in large serif font (white)
- Author attribution below (gold)
- "The Stoic Seller" logo bottom right
- Subtle column/pillar graphic elements

Example Design:
[Deep Blue Background]
"You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength."

— Marcus Aurelius

```

## Post Design Specifications

### Feed Posts
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1080px (square)
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Text**: Large enough to read on mobile
- **Branding**: Consistent logo placement

### Story Posts
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- **Safe Area**: Keep text/important elements in center
- **Format**: PNG, JPG, or MP4
- **Duration**: 15 seconds max for videos
- **Text**: Large, bold, high contrast

### Carousel Posts
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1080px per slide
- **Max Slides**: 10 (optimal: 5-7)
- **Consistency**: Same template across slides
- **Navigation**: Clear slide indicators
- **CTA**: Final slide with action

## Measurement & Optimization

### Track Visual Performance
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares
- **Reach**: How many people see your content
- **Saves**: Indicates valuable content
- **Profile Visits**: Traffic to your profile
- **Link Clicks**: Direct business impact

### A/B Testing Ideas
- **Color Schemes**: Blue vs. gold dominant
- **Text Placement**: Top vs. bottom
- **Image Style**: Professional vs. casual
- **Quote Length**: Short vs. long
- **Font Size**: Large vs. medium

### Monthly Review Process
1. Analyze top-performing posts
2. Identify visual patterns
3. Update templates based on insights
4. Refresh brand elements
5. Plan next month's visual strategy

This visual strategy ensures your Instagram presence is professional, consistent, and effective at driving traffic to your ebook and quiz while building authority in the sales training space.
