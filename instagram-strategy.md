## Daily Content Calendar Template

### Monday: Mindset Monday
**Format:** Carousel post with Stoic quote + sales application
**Example:** "What we can control in sales: Our preparation, our presence, our response. What we cannot: The prospect's decision, their timeline, their budget."

### Tuesday: Technique Tuesday
**Format:** Video or carousel explaining a specific sales technique
**Example:** "The Stoic Seller's approach to handling objections - using Amor Fati"

### Wednesday: Wisdom Wednesday
**Format:** Educational post with actionable advice
**Example:** "3 ways to practice Premeditatio Malorum before your next sales call"

### Thursday: Transformation Thursday
**Format:** Before/after story or case study
**Example:** "How Sarah went from pushy to present using Stoic principles"

### Friday: Friday Fundamentals
**Format:** Quick tip or principle recap
**Example:** "The Dichotomy of Control in 60 seconds"

### Saturday: Success Saturday
**Format:** Success story or testimonial
**Example:** "This week's wins from our Stoic Seller community"

### Sunday: Sunday Reflection
**Format:** Thought-provoking question or reflection prompt
**Example:** "What would Marcus Aurelius do in your next sales meeting?"
