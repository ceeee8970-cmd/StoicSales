⚡ QUICK TIP: MIRRORING WITHOUT MANIPULATION

Traditional sales: "Mirror their body language to build rapport"

Stoic sales: "Mirror their words to show understanding"

EXAMPLE:
Them: "We're concerned about the implementation timeline"
You: "I hear that timeline is a real concern for you. Tell me more about what 'ideal timing' looks like."

This shows you're listening, not just waiting to speak.
